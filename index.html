Darkvision

Darkvision changes exclude the following races

  • Aasimar
  • Dark Elf
  • Deep Gnome
  • Dwarf
  • Owlin
  • Tabaxi
  • Tiefling
  • Most Monsters
  • Let me know if you think your race should be excluded


Darkvision is now Low-Light Vision

Low-lightvision
  • You can see in dim light twice as far
    •
  • Dim Light within 20 feet is normal light
    •


Example

  • Human (no low-light vision) with a torch can see 20ft of light + 20ft of dim light.
  • Half-orc (low-light vision) with a torch can see 20ft of light + 40ft of dim light
